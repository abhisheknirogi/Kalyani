## üí° SEO TIPS FOR SERVICE-BASED BUSINESS

### Why Local SEO is Critical:
- 76% of people searching for salon services do it on mobile
- "Near me" searches have increased 200%+
- Local businesses get 50% more phone calls
- Google Business Profile appears in Local Pack (top 3 results)

### Your Competitive Advantage:
- Specialized niche (Saree draping + beauty)
- Specific location (Yelahanka)
- Direct contact options (WhatsApp, Phone)
- Portfolio/Gallery proof

### Best Practices:
1. **Consistency**: Use same business name/address everywhere
2. **Reviews**: A score of 4.5+ significantly improves rankings
3. **Keywords**: Use natural language, not keyword stuffing
4. **Mobile-First**: 60%+ traffic is mobile
5. **Speed**: Every 1s delay = 7% conversion loss
6. **Engagement**: Regular posts and updates boost visibility
